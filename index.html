Step 1

A Trigger
Is Something That Happens

This is the starting point of any automation.
Without a trigger, Zapier does nothing.

📧

New Email

An email arrives in your inbox

📝

Form Submitted

Someone fills out your form

👤

New Customer

A customer signs up

Always Watching

Zapier Is Always
Watching Your Apps

Think of Zapier like a system that monitors your apps in the background. As soon as something happens, it captures that event instantly.

👁️

Real-Time Detection

Zapier polls your apps every 1–15 minutes (or instantly with webhooks) to catch the moment your trigger fires.
