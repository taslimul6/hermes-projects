What Is It?

What Is Workflow Automation?

Workflow Automation is the process of using software to automatically complete tasks, move information between apps, and trigger actions based on specific rules.

Create it once.

The system handles it forever.

How It Works

The Difference

Manual Work vs Automation

A customer fills out your contact form. Here's what happens:

😓 Without Automation

  • Manually check the form
  • Send a reply yourself
  • Add customer to CRM manually
  • Notify your team manually
  • Repeat for every single lead
VS

⚡ With Automation

  • Form triggers workflow automatically
  • Welcome email sent instantly
  • CRM updated automatically
  • Team notified in real-time
  • All done in seconds — every time
